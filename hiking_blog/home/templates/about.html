About Tamarack Treks

Tamarack Treks is an online resource and community that aims to help you explore Western Montana in the best way possible. Western Montana is home to myriad camping locations, hiking trails and recreational destinations -- it can be overwhelming to narrow down where you want to explore. With community reviews and local tips, we will help you find the right trails, camping spots, and gear you need to have a great time.

Become a registered user for free. Users can add their favorite gear, trails and campsites to their profile. Contribute to the database by uploading your favorite trail and campsite photos. We appreciate any and all contributions to the site.
